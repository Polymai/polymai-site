Requirements

Bring your own AI and provider accounts

Polymai currently runs as a VS Code extension. You need VS Code installed, plus your own AI and provider accounts depending on what you build. You keep ownership of your OpenAI, ChatGPT/Codex, Supabase, GitHub, Stripe, Resend, and other provider accounts. Polymai subscriptions do not include third-party provider usage.
